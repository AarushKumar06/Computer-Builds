Minimum Requirements for Laptop

  • Processor - Intel i5 12450HX or similar. AMD ryzen 5 7235HS, AMD ryzen 7 7435HS or similar
  • Graphic Card - RTX 4060 6gb ram OR RTX 4050 6gb Preferably but RTX 3050 works fine too
  • RAM - 16gb DDR5
  • Storage - must've two nvme slots, 1tb
  • TDP - atleast 75W (worst case scenario then aboev 60W)
  • Display - 144HZ with great colours, for ex. display of Lenovo LOQ
