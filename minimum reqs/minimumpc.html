Minimum Requirements

  • Processor - Intel i5 12400F or i5 13400F
  • Motherboard - As per the reqs
  • Graphics Card - RTX 4060 8gb or RX 7600 8gb OR RX 6600xtx 8gb
  • RAM - 1x16gb DDR5 or 2x8gb DDR4
  • Storage - nvme 500gb x2 OR nvme 500gb + 500gb Sata
  • PSU - 80 plus bronze and 550W
  • Cooler - Coolermaster Hyper 212 spectrum v3
  • UPS - according to pc input wattage
